About Civic Catalyst

Civic Catalyst is a professional leadership platform focused on empowering citizens, with youth as the main drivers of change. We aim to transform potential into power, ideas into action, and vision into leadership.

Our Mission

To unlock the potential of every citizen by providing resources, guidance, and a platform for leadership, fostering ethics, innovation, and societal responsibility.

Our Vision

To create a future where all citizens are empowered to contribute meaningfully, youth lead with integrity, and Bangladesh stands as a model of sustainable development, social responsibility, and innovation.
